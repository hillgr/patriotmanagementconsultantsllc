Services

Project Owner’s Representative

  • We are the owner’s advocate.
  • Experienced professionals whose role is to act solely and exclusively in the interest of the client
  • Support the decision making process for the best possible construction project​
  • Handle complex issues to free up the owner’s time to managing and build their business while assuring the construction project is delivered as planned
  • Maximize the chances for a trouble-free project
     
     

 

Preconstruction Services​

  • Facilitate building the BEST team of professionals for your unique needs
  • Work with your management team to establish objectives, realistic goals, and milestone schedules
  • Review bid documents to buyout the project at the best value to improve constructability, minimize change orders and discrepancies
  • Evaluate bids, recommend contract awards, and review construction contracts
  • Develop value engineering ideas and incorporate into the process as alternatives during the bid

 

Establish Goals and Requirements

  • As your representative, we can recommend experienced Architects, Engineers, and Designers
  • We combine years of experience with a commitment to meet your needs through all facets of the project
  • We are at our best when involved from the onset of a project. The earlier the involvement, the smother the process

 

Construction Management

  • Be your professional eyes and ears on site, your advocate, ensuring your interests are maintained
  • Coordinate with the general contractor, major subcontractors, and material suppliers
  • Make certain EVERYONE understands the complete project requirements
  • Identify problems early, evaluate and manage the problem, and recommend solutions
  • Review progress and performance to assure the schedule is being maintained​

 

Critical Path Scheduling

  • Identify milestones to be met
  • Understand and explain the full impact of delays
  • Identify issues early so that expectations can be managed / problems mitigated
  • Coordinate and update agreement with the general contractor’s schedule
  • Schedule Owner’s move-in requirements with general contractor and local jurisdiction for a Certificate of Occupancy
